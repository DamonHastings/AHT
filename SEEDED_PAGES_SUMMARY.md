## 💡 Pro Tips

### Content
- The sample text is realistic—use it as a template
- All CTAs point to `#contact`—update when you have a contact form
- Quotes are inspirational—replace with your own or keep them

### Images
- Hero sections support background images
- Philosophy sections support side images
- Upload high-res images (1920px+ width)
- Use overlays to ensure text readability

### Layout
- Spacers are crucial—they create breathing room
- Use "medium" spacers between sections
- Use "large" spacers before major sections or CTAs
- Don't be afraid to reorder components

### Performance
- Images are automatically optimized by Sanity
- All components are lazy-loaded
- Pages render quickly
